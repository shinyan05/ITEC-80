But First, Coffee in Imus

  • Post by Michael Oracion
  • June 5, 2022

With their tagline "Because good coffee doesn't have to be expensive, we are But First, Coffee.", I can say that they really live up to that. Their prices ranges from 30 pesos to 450 pesos which is a 1 Liter Bottle of their House Special.

Address: 67A General Yengcon Nueno Avenue 4103 Imus, Philippines

Store Hours:
10:00 AM to 8:00 PM, Monday to Wednesday & Friday to Sunday.
10:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Thursday.

This Blueberry Cheesecake is to die for!! It has a smooth consistency and it's not too sweet. It's really really yummy and it goes well with the iced spanish latte. One order is perfect for sharing.

The Iced Spanish Latte is a perfect refresher in the middle of a busy day. I'm quite picky with my iced coffee but this one has easily became a favorite! So happy i found this friendly neighborhood coffee shop!

For my overall experience, This place is very great! I really like their interior design with wood and white. For the staffs, they are so friendly and accomodating, they respond quickly to my messages on facebook.
