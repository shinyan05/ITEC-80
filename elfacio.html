El Facio Kawayanan Café in Maragondon

  • Post by Ethia Chaina Lacsa
  • May 28, 2022

El Facio Kawayanan Café is a place where you can sip coffee, sit back and relax while enjoying the view.

They offer a wide variety of foods, drinks and pastries!

Address: El Facio Kawayanan Café, Pantihan Kawayanan Resort, Pantihan 2 (Sagbat), Maragondon, Philippines

Store Hours:
Monday - Friday: 1:00 PM to 8:00 PM
Saturday & Sunday : 7:00 AM to 10:00 AM & 1:00 PM to 8:00 PM.

The Caramel Macchiato in this cafe is a bit different for my taste, its more bitter than the one i like, but all in all i still love it because it's coffee!

They also have Korean Garlic Buns which i also like because i love garlic so much!

For my overall experience, El Facio Kawayanan Café is a relaxing place because it is inside a resort. They also have jamming sessions every night. They have great tasting foods and drinks, and their staff are accomodating.
