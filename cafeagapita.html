Cafe Agapita in Silang, Cavite

  • Post by Ethia Chaina Lacsa
  • May 28, 2022

Cafe Agapita is a family-run cafe that offers freshly brewed local coffee and homemade cakes.

Cafe Agapita is situated at the heart of Silang, Cavite. Our events space is teeming with lush greenery of all sorts and enveloped in the region’s freshest air, creating the perfect getaway spot for your next outing or event.

Address: 11 Kapitan Sayas St. Sabutan, Silang, Philippines

How to get there: Kindly take a bus bound to Tagaytay/ Nasugbu (If from Manila/Bacoor/Imus/Dasma) or bus bound to Dasma (if from Tagaytay). Ask the conductor to drop you off at Estrella Hospital. From there, take a tricycle going to Cafe Agapita.

Landmarks: Beside Nanay's Catering, in front of Sabutan's Barangay Hall

They are open from Monday to Sunday, 7:00 AM to 9:00 PM.

We have tried their Creamy Mushroom for 250 pesos, and what i can say is its very creamy and the serving size is good for 2-3 persons.

Next, we have the Caramel Macchiato for 165 pesos. In personal opinion, i really like the taste, its sweet and creamy.

Then, we have the Java Chip Frappe for 165 pesos. I like the little chocolate chip chunks on the bottom. It taste like a mocha frappuccino.

Most of their drinks cost around 80 to 165 pesos. For their food, it cost around 65 to 355 pesos.

For my overall experience, Cafe Agapita is a relaxing place to go to. I love the ambiance of the place and it is also eco-friendly. They have great tasting foods and drinks. Most of their food are in generous servings.Staff are warm and accomodating, they will greet you from the moment you entered the cafe and will accompany you to available seats of your choice.
